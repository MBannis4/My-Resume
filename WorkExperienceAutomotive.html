AUTOMOTIVE INDUSTRY - Aftersales Department

What Is the Automotive Industry's aftersales department?
  • The after-sales department in the automotive industry focuses on services and support provided to customers after they purchase a vehicle. This includes maintenance, repairs, spare parts, warranty services, and customer support. Its goal is to ensure customer satisfaction, maintain vehicle performance, and build long-term relationships with customers, often generating additional revenue for automotive companies.
